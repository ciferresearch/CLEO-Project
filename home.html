Welcome to the Home Page

The SDSP is a healthcare data management prototype specifically developed to securely store, facilitate access to, and enable the sharing of patients' 'omic data for precision oncology initiatives.

Key features of SDSP include:

  • Data Security: The SDSP utilizes decentralized blockchain technology and a distributed storage system to ensure data security.
  • User centric Approach: The SDSP prioritizes user consent and control by allowing patients to grant or revoke data access, ensuring they have full authority over their information.
  • Privacy: The SDSP incorporates privacy-enhancing technologies to ensure the protection of sensitive patient information while facilitating its utilization within the Learning Health System (LHS)
